# 🧍 About Me

Hi, I’m Matthew. I’m a software engineer and escape room builder.

You can find my work at [matthewmercado.dev](https://www.matthewmercado.dev/).

### Background

I studied Computer Science at Mount St. Mary’s University. I also spent years working in escape rooms as a designer and operator.

At Escape This Frederick, I built and managed [escapethisfrederick.com](https://escapethisfrederick.com/). Over 8 years, 110+ games played, and several rooms designed. I kept running into the same ops problem: great rooms, messy tooling.

I also built [lvlupvr.com](https://lvlupvr.com/) for Level Up VR. It hosts VR escape room experiences.

### What I’m building

* **Escape Director:** a web app for designing rooms, running sessions, and tracking outcomes.

Escape Director started as my senior capstone project. Now it’s a real product focused on faster onboarding, richer clue delivery, and data-driven decisions.
